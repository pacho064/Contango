About Contango Energy

Contango Energy is a Middle East-focused energy intelligence project built for people who need clear, source-linked market context across crude pricing, infrastructure constraints, trade direction, and risk.

Who this is for
Audience
Analysts, traders, policy researchers, journalists, and students tracking regional oil and energy dynamics.
Core lens
Middle East first, then global context (US, Russia, China, benchmark markets).
Goal
Explain what moved, why it moved, and which chokepoints or policy signals matter next.
Free now vs premium later
Free tier
Core 5 headline series, baseline event comparisons, public methodology, and basic map overlays.
Future premium tier
Deeper scenario tools, richer country sheets, route stress scoring, and expanded product breakdowns.
Principle
Core definitions and methodology remain visible; premium adds depth, not opacity.
Editorial standards
Provenance
Every headline number should have a source label and (when available) a source URL.
Cadence clarity
Release-driven indicators are labeled as official cadence, not marketed as live ticks.
Conflict sensitivity
Event framing uses explicit dates and transparent baseline assumptions.
No financial advice
This platform is a research/intelligence product, not an investment recommendation service.
Current development phase: foundational product build (public). Immediate roadmap is reliability, source coverage expansion, and tighter country-level intelligence.
