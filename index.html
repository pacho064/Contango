The Yanbu constraint: Saudi Arabia's East-West pipeline carries up to 5M bbl/d from Abqaiq to Yanbu on the Red Sea — widely cited as the solution to Strait of Hormuz vulnerability. But Yanbu's export capacity is only ~1.8M bbl/d. Houthi operations at Bab el-Mandeb add a second chokepoint to this already constrained bypass route.
