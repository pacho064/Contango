Methodology

Prices are compiled from publicly available NOC disclosures — Saudi Aramco, ADNOC, SOMO, QatarEnergy OSP releases. Spreads are calculated against the ICE Brent front-month contract. All figures are indicative and updated when official releases are published.

Risk classifications use US State Department Travel Advisory levels and ACLED armed conflict data. Tiers: Low / Moderate / High. No proprietary scoring methodology is used.

This platform is not financial advice. Source code: github.com/pacho064/Contango.
