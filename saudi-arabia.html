Price reality

Official Selling Prices and the Brent gap

Saudi Aramco publishes monthly OSPs for each grade, set as a differential to the Oman/Dubai average for Asian customers. The spread to ICE Brent is the market's real signal on Middle East sour crude demand and supply constraints.

Infrastructure

Terminals, pipelines and processing capacity

Saudi Arabia's oil infrastructure is extraordinarily concentrated. The majority of crude processing passes through a single facility — Abqaiq — before reaching export terminals, nearly all of which are located on the Arabian Gulf coast and dependent on Hormuz transit.
